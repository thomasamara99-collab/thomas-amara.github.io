THOMAS AMARA

Welcome to my personal website. My name is Thomas. Currently a second-year business management student at the university of Southampton, my passion for learning new things, building new skills, and a constant need to keep myself busy have led me to pursue many interests outside of the classroom...

This website serves as a space to document my work, research, and ideas. It brings together the projects I have built, the questions I explore through research, and the interests that shape the way I think about finance, markets, and innovation.

Projects

A collection of analytical and entrepreneurial projects exploring finance and business strategy - always blended with a bit of technology. Each project reflects an attempt to solve a problem, learn a new skill, or build something new.

Research

Research exploring financial markets, macroeconomics, and quantitative analysis. This section includes academic papers, research projects, and ongoing projects. The research I am most proud of is my US bond market stress index using HMM and UMAP.

Investing

My investing philosophy, theses, and reflections. Focused on disciplined analysis, risk management, and patient conviction over short-term noise.
